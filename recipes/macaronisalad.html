"Mac Salad"

Backstory

Macaroni Salad is such an easyside to prepare. It goes with everything from Barbeque, to hamburgers, to vegetarian meals too!
It is a refreshing, extremely filling cold or hot salad. I personally don't ever want it hot, but my girlfriend Lori swears by it as a hot salad.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 - 1 lb. of your favorite pasta
  • 3/4 cup of your favorite mayonnaise, I suggest Hellman's
  • 5-6 cups of water
  • 2/3 cup of diced celery, or 1 tsp of celery seed
  • Optional: 2 large hard-boiled eggs
  • 1/3 cup diced red, or white, or green onion
  • Optional: 1/2 cup finely diced bell pepper
  • 2 tbsp pickle juice
  • 1 tbsp red wine, or white, or apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp white or brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp mustard (they call for dijon, but not for me)
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/3 tsp garlic powder
  • Optional: 1/3 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 1 tsp of olive oil
  • Optional: 2-4 diced pickles

Cookware and Utensils Required

  • Medium-large pot
  • Mixing spoon
  • Spatula
  • Chef or paring knife
  • Medium mixing bowl
  • Set of measuring cups

Steps

  1. Wash and dry produce.
  2. Prepare macaroni or pasta according to the package.
  3. Drain and rinse macaroni in cold water, drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Combine dry seasonings and wet ingredients in mixing bowl.
  5. Slice vegetables for salad.
  6. Assemble all ingredients in mixing bowl. Mix well.
  7. I recommend chilling for at least an hour before serving.
