"Slowcooker Beef"

Backstory

Slowcooker Beef is an amazing and easy to throw together meal. My girlfriend Lori introduced me to it, and I've been in love ever since. It is delicious over noodles, rice, or really any starch you can imagine. This rich meat-filled sauce takes just 3 ingredients that are in most people's larders and fridges, and has such a rich result.

Ingredients

  • 1- 1 lb. Round Steak, or any steak that will be tender
  • 1- 2 oz. Lipton "Recipe Secrets" Onion Soup & Dip packet
  • 1- 10.5 oz. Campbell's "Cream of Mushroom" can

Cookware and Utensils Required

  • 5 oz. or bigger crockpot/slowcooker w/lid
  • Service spoon or ladle
  • Can opener
  • Fork to test the cook on the beef

Steps

  1. Wash your 1 pound of beef, and cut(optional)
  2. Open 10.5 oz. can of Cream of Mushroom and 2 oz. Lipton Onion seasoning packet.
  3. Place beef, cream of mushroom, and onion season packet into slowcooker.
  4. Turn slowcooker knob to "low", and come back in 6 hours.
  5. Optionally: Turn slowcooker knob to "high" and be done in 4 hours!
